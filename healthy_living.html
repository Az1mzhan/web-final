Healthy Living

At Fast Food Health, we are committed to promoting a lifestyle that prioritizes well-being and healthy living. Explore the various aspects of maintaining a balanced and nutritious life on this page.

Count calories

Daily calorie intake of a statistically average human is approximately 2500 calories, which is equal to the energy, released by burning 400 grams of a coal. However, it is widely known that fast food is primarily high-calorie food. To avoid calorie surplus, it would be better to start counting your daily calorie intake. You can easily evaluate daily calorie intake using the calculator below.

Establish food culture

Socrates once said: “You are what you eat”. Beside amount of calories, you also have to look after the quality of meals that you consume. Instead of fast food that contains a plethora of food additives, harmful cholesterol and complex carbs it would be more appropriate to add meals that have perfect proportions of fast carbs, proteins and fats.
